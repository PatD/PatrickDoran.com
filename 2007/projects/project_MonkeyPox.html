Client: Artist Marcot Badot
Project: MonkeyPox Multimedia Presentation

My Role UI Creation, Flash Actionscripting & CD Production

A Local artist needs to show off hundreds of his collage images, with synced sound, in an installation kiosk. He was showing this piece all over Europe, so he had to be sure it would work perfectly on multiple unknown computers with varying screen sizes and operating systems.

Using a mix of Flash, Action Scripting, and XML I was able to get it all together in time for his shows.
