Client: Datacorp
Project: Adult Treatment Planning Chart

My Role Design & Production

Datacorp came to me with a large PDF chart, with a large table and many maps that they had used a poster for a client demonstration. They needed a smart way to add this to their site.

I knew that a large PDF of the poster wasn't the way to go, so I broke the data down into chunks and put it into a single window where the user can see a lot of information in a small space.

I used the Lightbox script to display the maps, and that helped keep the presentation clean and tidy.
