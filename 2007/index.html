About Patrick

Hi there. Thanks for checking in. I'm a freelance web and multimedia designer located in North Carolina. This site is a catalog of my design work.

I enjoy being creative and all the components related to making websites. Great websites. Sites with style, and Cascading Style Sheets. I also enjoy print and multimedia work, when the opportunity presents itself. I've even been known to do a little PowerPoint work, and dabble in Excel whenever it is right for the project.

Getting your elements organized in a meaningful way is such an important part of what I do. The design challenge I enjoy the most is finding the right way to get your content and your message displayed.

For me, this whole web thing started in 1997 when I started making sites in Netscape Composer for friends and family. Almost 10 years later I'm still doing what I love (although not in Composer anymore) and learning new techniques and technologies all the time.

I reside with the wife and the 2.5 cats just outside of Raleigh, NC. If your curious about what's on my mind, please check out: Rockland-ave.com where my wife and I blog regularly.

So, what can do I do for you?

  • Website Design, Production & Construction

  • Print and Graphic Design: Flyers, Posters, PDFs.
  • PowerPoint Design
  • Excel Work, including VBA
  • Hardware installation & Other Nerdy Things
